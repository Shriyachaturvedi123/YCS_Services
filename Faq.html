Frequently Asked Question

Here’s an example for a Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) section with toggle functionality:

What services does Yes Courier offer?
Yes Courier offers a wide range of logistics services, including container shipping (FCL and LCL), air delivery, ground shipping, train delivery, warehousing, and distribution. We also specialize in project cargo and break-bulk transportation.
How can I track my shipment?
You can track your shipment in real-time by using our online tracking tool on the website. Simply enter your tracking number or shipment details, and you'll get up-to-date information on your cargo's location and status.
How do I get a quote for shipping?
To receive a quote, you can fill out our online request form or contact our customer service team. Provide details like shipment size, origin, destination, and preferred shipping method, and we’ll send you a tailored quote.
What if my item is damaged during shipping?
If your item is damaged, please contact customer service immediately. We'll guide you through the claims process and work to resolve the issue, including providing insurance options for covered losses.
How do I contact customer service?
You can reach our customer service team through the "Contact Us" page on our website, via email, or by calling our dedicated support line. We are available to assist you with any queries or concerns.
